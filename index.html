    Who we are

    Come as you are. Grow together in Christ.

    Newark Christian Fellowship is a community of ordinary people following Jesus. We gather each week to worship, to study the Scriptures, and to care for one another and our city.

    Whether you have followed Christ for decades or are exploring faith for the first time, you are welcome at our table.
